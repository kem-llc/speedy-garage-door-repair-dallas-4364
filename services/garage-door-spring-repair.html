Garage Door Spring Repair

Expert torsion and extension spring replacement for safe, smooth operation.

Schedule this service

Overview

Broken springs are the most common garage door issue and one of the most dangerous to fix. Our technicians safely replace torsion and extension springs with precision tools and high-quality springs rated for thousands of cycles. We ensure proper tension and balance so your door operates smoothly and quietly.

What's included

  • Restores safe operation
  • Prevents further damage
  • Extends door lifespan
  • Reduces noise and vibration

How it works

1

Inspection

We assess the spring type, condition, and door weight to select the correct replacement.

2

Safe Removal

Using winding bars and safety protocols, we remove the old spring without risk.

3

Installation & Test

New spring is installed, tensioned, and tested for balance and smooth travel.

Common questions

How long do garage door springs last?

Typically 7-10 years depending on usage. We use high-cycle springs for longer life.

Can I replace a spring myself?

No — spring replacement is extremely dangerous due to high tension. Always hire a professional.
