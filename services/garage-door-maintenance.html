Garage Door Maintenance

Keep your garage door running smoothly with our preventive maintenance service.

Schedule this service

Overview

Regular maintenance extends the life of your garage door and prevents costly breakdowns. Our comprehensive maintenance visit includes lubrication of all moving parts, tightening of hardware, testing of safety features, and inspection of springs, cables, and rollers. We also check the opener's performance and adjust settings as needed. Schedule twice-yearly maintenance for optimal reliability.

What's included

  • Extends door lifespan by up to 5 years
  • Prevents unexpected breakdowns
  • Improves safety and quiet operation
  • Includes a detailed service report

How it works

1

Visual Inspection

We examine all components for wear, rust, or damage, including springs, cables, rollers, and tracks.

2

Lubrication & Adjustment

We lubricate hinges, springs, and rollers, and adjust tension and opener settings for smooth operation.

3

Safety Test

We test auto-reverse, photo eyes, and manual release to ensure your door meets safety standards.

Common questions

How often should I have my garage door serviced?

We recommend professional maintenance at least once a year, or twice a year for heavily used doors.

Can I do maintenance myself?

Basic tasks like visual checks and lubrication are DIY-friendly, but we recommend professional service for safety-critical adjustments.
