Our story

Your Local Garage Door Experts

To provide Dallas homeowners with fast, affordable, and reliable garage door service they can trust.

Speedy Garage Door Repair was founded in 1998 by a group of experienced technicians who saw a need for honest, prompt service in the Dallas area. What started as a small operation with one truck has grown into a trusted local business serving thousands of homeowners. We've built our reputation on transparency — no hidden fees, no unnecessary repairs, just quality work at a fair price.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of certified technicians who live and work in the communities they serve. We invest in ongoing training and top-of-the-line equipment to ensure every job is done right the first time. Whether it's a minor adjustment or a full door replacement, we treat your home with the same care we would our own.
